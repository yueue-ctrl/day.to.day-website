# day.to.day-website
d.T.d - independent design studio
• identity, digital products & systems
• powered by data, technology & custom code
• based in Hangzhou & Boston

The d.T.d website is developed from the structure of its logo — a small rotating pinwheel symbolizing the continuous motion of day-to-day practice. The site is designed as a bilingual platform in Chinese and English.

The logo also acts as the interaction trigger for the interface. The horizontal bar of the letter “T” functions as a visual window that divides the screen into two sections. As the letter “d” rotates, when placed horizontally it momentarily transforms into the letter “p,” structurally organizing the content into two categories on the left and right: Play and Pay.

The Play section represents the members’ everyday explorations and experiments, corresponding to the Chinese phrase “在玩” (playing). The Pay section presents commercial or commissioned projects, corresponding to “在忙” (busy working).

Selecting either Play or Pay expands the corresponding page. At the same time, the logo rotates back to a vertical orientation, returning to its original “dTd” form.

Works on the website can also be browsed through two classification systems. One is by approach, including categories such as Interaction, Book, and Graphic. The other is by year, allowing visitors to navigate projects chronologically from 2025, 2024, 2023, to 2022.
